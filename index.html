Sam Farley: Web Designer & Developer

Welcome to my portfolio!

After recently graduating from the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire with a degree in multimedia and web design, I am looking to enter the workforce as a web developer.

I have dual interests in design and development and believe each cannot exist without the other. I am passionate about using languages—both human and computer—to better connect the world and its people. Ultimately, my goal is to strengthen global communication by improving the virtual landscape of an international company.

Note that much of my existing work is in the realm of multimedia; I am excited to gain more experience in web development and am in the process of doing so.
